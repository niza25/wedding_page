How to get to Sokolka?

If you are not coming by car, the best way to get to Sokolka is to first fly to Warsaw Chopin Airport (Okecie), or take a train to Warsaw Central Station (Warszawa Centralna).

From the airport you can rent a car (the best way) or take a direct bus to Bialystok (PlusBus, ZakExpress- c. 3,5ha) and than a bus or train to Sokolka (c. 1ha).

If you arrive at Warsaw Central Station (Warszawa Centralna), there is a bus, or a train to Bialystok (or directly to Sokolka). However, there is not too many, so plan in advance or ask us for help. We are always happy to help you.

    From Warsaw Chopin Airport (Okecie) to Bialystok (Dworzc Biacomex - Central Station):
  • PlusBus
  • ZakExpress
    From Bialystok (Dworzc Biacomex - Central Station) to Sokolka:
  • by bus
  • by train
